Pokémon List

Learn about the first 20 Pokémon!

1. Bulbasaur

#001

A Grass/Poison-type Pokémon known for the plant bulb on its back.

2. Ivysaur

#002

A Grass/Poison-type evolution of Bulbasaur, with a larger flower on its back.

3. Venusaur

#003

The final evolution of Bulbasaur, featuring a massive flower on its back.

4. Charmander

#004

A Fire-type Pokémon that resembles a small dinosaur with a flame on its tail.

5. Charmeleon

#005

The Fire-type evolution of Charmander, with increased strength and aggression.

6. Charizard

#006

The final evolution of Charmander, it becomes a powerful Fire/Flying-type dragon.

7. Squirtle

#007

A Water-type Pokémon resembling a small turtle, often seen with a playful attitude.

8. Wartortle

#008

The evolution of Squirtle, with larger ears and a tail that’s becoming more powerful.

9. Blastoise

#009

A large, turtle-like Pokémon with powerful water cannons on its back.

10. Caterpie

#010

A small Bug-type Pokémon, known for its distinctive green color and orange antennae.

11. Metapod

#011

The cocoon stage of Caterpie, where it prepares to evolve into Butterfree.

12. Butterfree

#012

A Bug/Flying-type Pokémon that evolves from Metapod and has large wings.

13. Weedle

#013

A small Bug/Poison-type Pokémon with a stinger on its head.

14. Kakuna

#014

The cocoon form of Weedle, known for being in a dormant state.

15. Beedrill

#015

A Bug/Poison-type Pokémon with sharp stingers and an aggressive nature.

16. Pidgey

#016

A small Normal/Flying-type bird Pokémon, often seen in forests and grasslands.

17. Pidgeotto

#017

The evolution of Pidgey, with sharper wings and a more powerful flying ability.

18. Pidgeot

#018

The final evolution of Pidgey, known for its speed and majestic appearance.

19. Rattata

#019

A small, rodent-like Normal-type Pokémon, often seen as a nuisance in urban areas.

20. Raticate

#020

The evolution of Rattata, with sharp teeth and a quick temper.
